FermentaCraft

Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 23, 2025

FermentaCraft is built to be fast, accurate, and organized. We keep your data simple: the app works offline-first, and you can optionally enable cloud sync. This page explains what information we handle and how.

Core Principles

  • Offline-first. Your recipes, batches, measurements, and inventory are stored locally on your device by default.
  • Sync is optional. You may enable cloud sync to back up and access data across devices. If you don’t enable it, your data stays on your device.
  • No ads. FermentaCraft does not show third‑party ads.

Data We Store

  • Local app data (on device): recipes, batches, ingredients, measurements, inventory, and settings (units, theme, etc.).
  • Cloud sync data (optional): if enabled, a copy of your brewing data is stored securely in our sync provider to keep it available across devices.
  • Purchase information: buying FermentaCraft Premium uses your app store account. We may receive non‑sensitive purchase status from the billing provider to unlock features.

Third‑Party Services

  • Sync/Storage: We may use trusted cloud services (e.g., Firebase) to provide optional sync and backups.
  • Payments: Purchases are processed by the app store (e.g., Google Play) and/or a subscription vendor (e.g., RevenueCat). We do not receive your full payment details.
  • Diagnostics: We may use crash/error reporting to improve app stability. These tools collect technical information about the app and device (e.g., OS version, crash stack traces).

Your Choices

  • Use offline only: keep sync disabled and your data remains on your device.
  • Export/Import: use the app’s data export/import to create backups or move devices.
  • Delete data: you can remove local data from within the app. If you use sync, contact us to request deletion of cloud copies linked to your account.

Children’s Privacy

FermentaCraft is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach out at support@fermentacraft.com.

This policy may change as the app evolves. We’ll update the date above when we make changes.
