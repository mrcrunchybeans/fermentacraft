FermentaCraft — Request Account & Data Deletion

Developer: Brian Petry
App: FermentaCraft

This page explains how you can request deletion of your FermentaCraft account and the data associated with it.

How to request deletion

  1. In-app (recommended): Open the app → Settings → Account → Delete Account. Follow the confirmation prompts. This deletes your account and associated cloud data.
  2. Web request: If you can’t access the app, email privacy@fermentacraft.com.com from the email address you use to sign in. Include the subject line Account Deletion – FermentaCraft. We’ll verify your identity and process the deletion.
Identity verification: For security, deletion requests must come from the signed-in account in-app or from the same email address used for your account.

What we delete

  • Account credentials (Firebase Auth user).
  • User-generated data stored in our cloud (e.g., recipes, batches, measurements, inventory, notes) in Cloud Firestore.
  • Linked cloud files stored by us (if any) in Cloud Storage.
  • Purchase entitlement link between your account and RevenueCat (if applicable) is removed/anonymized on deletion.

What may be retained

  • App Store purchase records (Google Play / Apple) are maintained by the stores and can’t be deleted by us. These records don’t allow access to your account in our app.
  • Backups/Logs: Minimal disaster-recovery backups may persist for up to 30 days and are automatically purged. We do not use these for any purpose other than recovery.
  • Legal/Compliance: If required by law, we may retain limited information necessary to meet legal obligations.

Timing

Deletion is normally processed immediately after your request. Backup purges complete within 30 days.
