About FermentaCraft

FermentaCraft is the ultimate companion for home brewers and fermentation enthusiasts. Whether you’re making cider, mead, kombucha, or other craft beverages, our app helps you design recipes, track batches, and calculate key brewing metrics with ease.

Key Features

  • Recipe creation and management
  • Batch logging with notes and measurements
  • Built-in brewing tools and calculators
  • Inventory tracking for ingredients
  • Cloud sync to keep your data safe
